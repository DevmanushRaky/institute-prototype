Private Data We Receive And Collect

Our institute automatically collects and receives certain information from your computer or mobile device, including the activities you perform on our website, learning platforms, and applications. This includes details about the type of hardware and software you are using (such as your operating system or browser) and information obtained from cookies. For example, each time you visit our website or use our services, we may collect your IP address, browser and device type, access times, the web page from which you arrived, your general location, and the pages you access on our site.

When you first register for an account with us or enroll in a course, we collect some Personal Information, such as:

  • Your geographic location may be collected to optimize course availability and ensure compliance with regional learning standards.
  • Your full name, username, email address, and other contact details should be provided in the registration and inquiry forms for smooth communication.
  • A unique student or faculty ID (assigned upon registration) will help us manage enrollments and provide personalized access.
  • All learning materials, student data, and system information are backed up regularly to ensure security and accessibility.
  • Your IP address and timestamps related to form submissions may be recorded to enhance security and verify access.
  • Additional information submitted by students, faculty, or representatives may be stored to facilitate course management and academic services.
  • Your billing address and other necessary details to process course registrations or payments securely. We may also collect payment details (e.g., credit card or PayPal) where applicable.
  • User-generated content such as messages, comments, posts, or profiles shared on our learning platform.
  • Images, assignments, or other files uploaded as part of course activities or student engagement.
  • Communication records, including inquiries, feedback, or support requests, to help us enhance your learning experience.

How We Use Your Data

Our institute is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect and use visitor data for the following purposes:

  1. To verify your identity when you log in and ensure a secure session.
  2. To operate our services efficiently and provide seamless access to our courses.
  3. To process registrations, verify transactions, and ensure secure payments.
  4. To analyze user engagement and improve our educational offerings.
  5. To provide support, respond to inquiries, and enhance user experience.
  6. To share non-identifiable statistics with education partners and stakeholders.
  7. To keep you informed about new courses, events, and institute updates.
  8. To market our training programs where permitted by law.
  9. To identify learning trends and improve course content and delivery.
  10. To ensure the security of our platform for students and faculty.
  11. To track website traffic and optimize user experience.
